Why choose Mobile Grooming

Looking for the best mobile pet grooming service? Choose Furry Land Mobile Pet Grooming for convenience, affordability, and peace of mind! Busy schedule? Not a problem! Our state-of-the-art mobile grooming salon comes to you! Furry Land Mobile Grooming team is equipped with the highest quality of training, producing the most compassionate and skilled groomers in the nation! We only use industry-leading equipment and the most gentle and effective grooming products available to give your dog or cat a stress-free, full-service grooming experience you can count on! Plus when your pet leaves our mobile grooming station they will be happy, clean, and refreshed — that’s the Furry Land promise!
